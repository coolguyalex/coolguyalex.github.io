Uradros takes 240 days to rotate around it's star.
Each Season is 60 days long, each month is 20 days long, and each week 10.
The primary moon of Uradros, Velyk, takes an entire season to complete a cycle.
Velyk's moon, Sarth, takes around 12 days.
