## Goblinoid Dungeon Mastering System *pocket*
Inspirational fantasy companion.
GDMS-*pocket* is an easy-to-use, minimal, and customizable system that DMs and creators can use alone or at the game table.
Inspired by random tables and generators, GDMS-*pocket* wants to guve you an NPC name when you need it but get out of the way when you don't. 

## Music Friend 
A virtual pet and mix-tape sharing system that rewards you for making custom recorded mixes by growing your digital audiophile hatchling. 
Music friend lets you record audio by acting as a bluetooth sink (and source), audio input jacks, and an on-board microphone. 
Add your recordings to playslists to create mixes to share with your friends.
Every recording you make, tag you edit, and playlist you share adds to your pet as it grows. 

## Brain builder 
Imagine a picture book that never ends and adjusts to your child's learning. 
Brain builder contains thousands of words, images, sounds, and concepts to show your childs developing mind the world. 
Brain builder leverages child pstchology and development and the idea of Spaced Repition Algorithms (SRS) to display novel content when it makes the most sense.
